Stock Management Information

Total Stocked Ever: The total number of items ever added to inventory.

Lost/Spoiled Items: The number of items that have been lost, damaged or spoiled. Enter a positive number when recording spoiled items - the system automatically subtracts them from your stock.

Cycle-based spaces: For experiences with limited weekly spaces, tick cycle mode and set the spaces per cycle, the number of weeks in the cycle (0 = every week), and the reset day. Spaces reset at 02:00 Europe/London on the chosen day, anchored to the most recent reset day.

Current Stock: Total-based: Total Stocked Ever - Lost/Spoiled Items - Purchased Items. Cycle-based: Spaces per cycle minus active purchases since the last reset.
