Stock Management Information

Total Stocked Ever: The total number of items ever added to inventory.

Lost/Spoiled Items: The number of items that have been lost, damaged or spoiled. Enter a positive number when recording spoiled items - the system automatically subtracts them from your stock.

Current Stock: Calculated as: Total Stocked Ever - Lost/Spoiled Items - Purchased Items
